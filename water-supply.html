Water Supply Notes

Introduction

Water Supply Engineering focuses on the design and management of systems to provide clean and safe water for domestic and industrial use.

Key Topics

  • Water Sources: Surface and groundwater sources.
  • Water Quality: Parameters and treatment processes.
  • Distribution Systems: Pipe networks and pumping stations.
  • Water Treatment: Filtration, disinfection, and softening.
  • Hydraulics: Flow calculations and pressure management.
