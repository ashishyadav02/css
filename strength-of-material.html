Strength of Material Notes

Introduction

Strength of Materials, also known as Mechanics of Materials, deals with the behavior of solid objects under various types of loading. This subject is fundamental for civil engineering, covering concepts like stress, strain, and material properties.

Key Topics

  • Stress and Strain: Definitions, types (normal, shear), and relationships.
  • Hooke’s Law: Elastic behavior and Young’s modulus.
  • Bending and Shear: Beam analysis, bending moment, and shear force diagrams.
  • Torsion: Twisting of shafts and polar moment of inertia.
  • Deflection: Beam deflection and slope calculations.
