Highway Engineering Notes

Introduction

Highway Engineering involves the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of roads and highways to ensure safe and efficient transportation.

Key Topics

  • Highway Planning: Alignment and geometric design.
  • Pavement Design: Flexible and rigid pavements.
  • Traffic Engineering: Traffic flow, signals, and safety.
  • Highway Materials: Asphalt, concrete, and aggregates.
  • Maintenance: Repair techniques and pavement management.
