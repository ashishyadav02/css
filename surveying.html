Surveying Notes

Introduction

Surveying involves measuring and mapping the earth’s surface to determine the position of points, distances, and angles for construction and planning.

Key Topics

  • Basic Principles: Triangulation, trilateration, and leveling.
  • Chain Surveying: Equipment and methods.
  • Theodolite Surveying: Angle measurement and traversing.
  • Leveling: Differential and profile leveling.
  • Modern Techniques: GPS and total station surveying.
